1. The Enterprise AI Infrastructure Challenge

Inline Governance: Built-In Data Quality Modern data product architectures embed governance directly into data processing, creating a unified approach where data products combine raw data with embedded rules, quality guarantees, and business context in a single, manageable entity.

Organizations need comprehensive data management platforms that use data products as fundamental building blocks. These platforms must handle semantic layer creation, maintenance, and evolution across hundreds of data sources and dozens of AI applications.
